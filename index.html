Why MediCard

Built for emergencies. Designed for clarity.

Instant Card Generation

Fill a simple form and generate a print-ready emergency health card in seconds. Downloadable as PDF or printable directly.

Critical Health Info

Store blood type, allergies, conditions, medications, and emergency contacts — all in a compact, standardized format.

Secure & Private

User accounts secured with Java Servlet sessions and server-side validation. Your data stays under your control.

Multi-Profile Support

Create and manage cards for your entire family. Perfect for elderly relatives, children, or dependants who need care.

Mobile First

Fully responsive across all devices. Access or present your card from any phone — no app download needed.

Java Servlet Backend

Built with a clear servlet architecture in mind. Easy to wire up registration, login, and form submission endpoints when ready.
